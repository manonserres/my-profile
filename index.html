Rugby

2 years ago, we created a female rugby team within IESEG. Members of the men's rugby team volunteered to teach us the basics and values of this team sport. From physical preparation to inter-school matches, we went to Barcelona to represent the IESEG at the World University Championship. Our progress and the assiduity of the coaches allowed us to remain motivated;

Volleyball

When I arrived at IESEG, we formed a new women's volleyball team. We progressed together thanks to girls who had previously played volleyball in clubs. We had the chance to participate in a championship in Rome.

Dance

I've been dancing since I was five. When I came to the IESEG, there was no dance club. In my second year, we decided to create a dance group and we participated in a competition in Rouen. Since then, this group has become an association and organizes workshops open every Thursday afternoon.
